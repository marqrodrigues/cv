I am currently a PhD student of the Molecular Biosciences doctoral program (iBET/ITQB-NOVA), working in the Cell-Based Vaccine Development Laboratory at the Animal Cell Technology Unit (ACT, Health & Pharma Division, iBET), and holder of a studentship granted by Fundação para a Ciência e Tecnologia. Current PhD research work is focused on the development of bioprocess engineering strategies for the improved production of ferritin-based vaccine candidates against infectious diseases.
